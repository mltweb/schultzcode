Depending on services you use, we collect data from one or more of these three sources:

When you request any of our services, we may ask, store, and process any/all of these data categories:

2. Cookies:

Cookies are small files placed on your computer when you first visit our website that helps analyze web traffic and tracks you across the web. The collection of this data is based on "implied consent", i.e we assume you agree to our terms of usage unless you explicitly opt out. You can opt out by:

  • Changing your browser settings to not accept cookies
  • Browsing in anonymous mode
  • Not using our website
